Emphasis Offline Editor v2.0.1 
Installation Instructions


Step 1: Install WYSIWYG Release 10 on a PC other than your Emphasis Server.

Step 2: Launch WYSIWYG and allow the libraries to complete their update.
	If requested, take this opportunity to register WYSIWYG.

Step 3: Exit WYSIWYG.

Step 4: Insert the Emphasis Offline Editor CD in the CD-ROM drive of your 
	computer, or download EOE from the ETC Web site, and double-click 
	the EOE Installer file.

Step 5: Follow the directions in the Emphasis Offline Editor installer.

Step 6: The installer will create a shortcut on your desktop and a program 
	group in Programs (i.e. 'Emphasis Offline Editor').
